Released Music

Drawing inspiration from his roots in Kansas City, Ceej Skylark captivates his fans with his contemporary, yet sonically-driven sound. His original but relatable lyrics impose strict attention yet invoke strict leisure. Throughout the struggles of growing up in East Side Kansas City, KS to dropping out of college and dodging the law, Skylark forces his way through life so fans can lay back and relax to a melancholy rendition of their own pent-up emotions.
